Daily UI challenge

In the summer of 2017, I started the daily UI challenge for 100 consecutive days. The reason to do this was to practice UI design & gain experience through tinkering, experimenting and a lot of trial & error. I believe that the quickest way to improve your craft is to just make a lot of stuff. This excercise not only improved my visual design skills but also helped me in making an eye for seeing patterns and understanding why those patterns are being used for certain contexts.
