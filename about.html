Hey there! I'm Rahul - a UX & Product Designer

I come from a background in electronics engineering, found my calling in design, and thrive in strategic, systems thinking. Currently, I'm pursuing Masters in Human-Computer Interaction at Indiana University, Indianapolis. Being a generalist I work on the entire design process from research and strategy to the final visual design and execution.

My philosophy is to create engaging & accessible user experiences that are also functional, clean & simple. I ask a lot of questions, always challenge my previous assumptions & care deeply about helping people grow.

Outside of work, you can often find me cycling, watching movies or photographing. I enjoy reading about psychology, design, technology and listening to an unhealthy amount of podcasts.

Design Process

01. Empathy

Learn about the users for whom you are designing the product or service.

02. Research

Gain a deep understanding of problems, behaviors and business goals.

03. Define

Gather insights & craft a meaningful & actionable problem statement.

04. Ideate

Brainstorm the widest possible range of ideas that can be implemented.

05. Prototype

Build interactive solution which brings you closer to the final product.

06. Evaluate

Test, collect feedback & learn more about your solution & the user.
